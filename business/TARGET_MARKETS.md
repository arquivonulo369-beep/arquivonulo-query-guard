# TARGET MARKETS
ArquivoNulo Global

QueryGuard targets environments where:

- Autonomous systems make consequential decisions
- Compliance must be verifiable
- Logs must be cryptographically anchored
- Governance cannot depend on narrative claims

Primary Markets:

1. AI Infrastructure Providers
   - LLM orchestration platforms
   - Agent frameworks
   - Autonomous system builders

2. Regulated Environments
   - Fintech
   - GovTech
   - HealthTech
   - Critical infrastructure systems

3. Security & Forensics
   - Audit frameworks
   - Incident validation
   - Cryptographic verification pipelines

4. Research & Experimental AGI Labs
   - Governance-before-execution architectures
   - Deterministic viability layers

Strategic Entry Point:

QueryGuard integrates as a middleware layer
before execution pipelines,
not after incident occurrence.
