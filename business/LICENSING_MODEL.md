# LICENSING MODEL
ArquivoNulo Global

QueryGuard adopts a dual-structure licensing strategy.

1. Open Integrity Core

- Core cryptographic verification components remain open.
- Merkle construction logic
- Snapshot validation
- Proof verification
- Signature validation routines

Purpose:
Ensure transparency and cryptographic trust.

2. Institutional Deployment Layer

Enterprise integrations, governance orchestration modules,
and compliance automation layers may be licensed separately.

Potential Models:

- Commercial deployment license
- Enterprise support agreements
- Institutional audit integration packages
- Compliance-as-infrastructure offerings

Strategic Principle:

Integrity must remain verifiable.
Operational scaling may be commercial.

Revenue Strategy:

Monetization derives from:
- Deployment integration
- Governance orchestration layers
- Institutional compliance pipelines
- Custom deterministic governance modules

No closed cryptographic claims.
Only open verifiable integrity + commercial deployment architecture.
